### Collaboration and Transfers
---

- Respect Support Boundaries.
    - Only collaborate or transfer cases to teams explicitly listed in the support boundaries.
    - If unsure, consult with a Technical Advisor (TA) or Partner Technical Advisor (PTA).
- Understand the Issue Thoroughly.
    - Confirm the customer ask and ensure the issue is well-scoped.
    - Avoid premature collaboration or transfers, engaging the wrong team wastes time and erodes customer trust.
- Document Everything.
    - Clearly document the reason for collaboration or transfer in the case notes.
    - If transferring due to timezone mismatch, ensure the customer has requested it and update the country/timezone, internal title, and queue accordingly.
- Track Collaboration Progress (If your collaboration has not been assigned or has been idle for a period of time, reach out to your TA/PTA or Technical Lead.)
    - After initiating a collaboration, follow the task to ensure visibility in the system view.
    - The SE must ensure the collaboration is assigned and progressing ownership of the parent case includes oversight of the collaboration.
    - Avoid Idle or Unassigned Collaborations.
- Track transfer progress. (If it has not been assigned within 24 hours, reach out to your TA/PTA or Technical Lead.)
    - It is important to ensure the transfer is assigned so that cases are properly handed over and delays are avoided; doing so will ensure that the customer has a good experience.
