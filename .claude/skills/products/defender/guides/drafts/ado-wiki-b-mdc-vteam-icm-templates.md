# MDC V-Team IcM Templates

## Purpose
Standardize and simplify CRI/ICM escalations by defining required information per VTeam area and category (feature).

## Goals
- Ensure consistent, high-quality ICMs
- Reduce back-and-forth between Support and CRI teams
- Speed up investigations by collecting the right data upfront

## Template Structure

Each sub VTeam page should follow this format:

### Page Title
`[Procedure] - V-MDC_<(area)>`

### Required Sections per Area

**Description of Issue**
- Detailed description including error messages, timestamps, and reproduction steps

**Customer Impact Assessment**
- Severity, number of affected users/subscriptions, and business impact

**Environment Fields Table**
| FieldName | Value | Requirement | Why Needed |
|-----------|-------|-------------|------------|
| (varies per area) | | Mandatory/Optional | |

**Ask**
- Specific action or resolution requested from the CRI team
