# Microsoft Defender for Cloud Known Issues Tracker

## Description
This page lists all active known issues to help track and manage ongoing work across products and features. Each entry includes the Work Item Type, Product Name, Feature, Title, Assigned Owner, and Current State.

**Before creating a new ICM request, please review this page to check if your customer is already impacted by a known issue.**
If the issue is listed here, there's no need to create a new ICM as you should find all the relevant details needed to communicate with your customer. This helps reduce duplicate requests to the Product Group (PG) teams and improves overall efficiency.

**If you do not find a matching known issue**, please review the [[Procedure] - IcM Escalations for CSS](https://dev.azure.com/ASIM-Security/Infrastructure%20Solutions/_wiki/wikis/Infrastructure%20Solutions/2457/-Procedure-IcM-Escalations-for-CSS) page to understand what is required by the PG team per service. Also, ensure that all requests are submitted via the [ICM Approval Tool](https://dev.azure.com/ASIM-Security/Infrastructure%20Solutions/_wiki/wikis/Infrastructure%20Solutions/10661/-Procedure-ICM-Approval-Tool).

**If you believe a known issue is missing from this list**, please follow the [[Guide] - Create a known issue item](https://dev.azure.com/ASIM-Security/Infrastructure%20Solutions/_wiki/wikis/Infrastructure%20Solutions/5555/-Guide-Create-a-known-issue-item) to ensure it is captured correctly and made visible to the broader team.
