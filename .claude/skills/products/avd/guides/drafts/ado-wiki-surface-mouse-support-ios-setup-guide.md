Step-by-Step: Enable Surface Mouse Support in Windows App on iOS
----------------------------------------------------------------

### Step 1: Open the Windows App on iOS

*   Launch the **Windows App** on the iOS device.
*   Confirm the **Favorites** screen is displayed.
*   This screen shows available **Cloud PCs** or **remote desktops**.

### Step 2: Open App Settings

*   In the top-right corner, select **Settings**.
*   This opens the **Windows App configuration menu**.

### Step 3: Navigate to Input Devices

*   From the **Settings** menu, select **Input Devices**.
*   This section lists **external input hardware** supported by the app.

### Step 4: Select Surface Mouse or Surface Arc Mouse

*   Under **Devices**, locate and select **Surface Mouse** or **Surface Arc Mouse**.
*   Selecting the device confirms that the **Windows App recognizes the mouse** as an available input device.
