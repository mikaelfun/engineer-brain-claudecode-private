# How to Share Logs in DTM Workspace with Dev/PG

When logs collected are bigger than the allowable limit in ICM, use DTM workspace to share with PG.

## Process

1. PG does not have access to DFM case but can access DTM URL.
2. Add PG/Dev directly to the DTM workspace.
3. Share DTM URL in the ICM for log access.
4. Reference guide for AVD (also applicable for W365 PG): [How to share customer data with AVD PG](https://dev.azure.com/Supportability/WindowsVirtualDesktop/_wiki/wikis/WindowsVirtualDesktop/820192/How-to-share-customer-data-with-AVD-PG)

## Key Notes

- Escort sessions are not transferable - if an Escort session is needed for further information, open an ICM which SaaF will escalate to Engineering.
- Engineering can open the Escort session and partner with support if information is needed from the customer.
