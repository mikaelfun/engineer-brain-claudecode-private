### **What is a POC?**

A POC is your primary contact within our team who can provide support or answer any questions related to their assigned customer. He has already an established relationship with the account team and is aware of all escalations and issues the customer is facing.

### **Do I transfer the case to the POC?**

No, the role of the POC is to centralize and facilitate the interaction with the customer, knowing what are the pain-points, already having an established relationship and knowing how the communications should work. Not all cases from that customer should be handled by the POC, but the POC needs to be aware of the case.

### **What is a CAM?**

A CAM is a manager from CSS, that is designated to help with the customer experience in CSS.

### **What is an MDO escalation manager?**

If you feel that the customer is not satisfied with the current experience he has, either with the Product or with the overall support experience, please follow the recovery call procedure and give notice to the MDO Escalation Manager, so they can be engaged in the recovery call.
