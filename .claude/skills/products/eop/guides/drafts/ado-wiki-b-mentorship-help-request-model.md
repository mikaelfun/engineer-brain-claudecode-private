# Mentorship Help Request Model

[[_TOC_]]


##Summary
We are introducing a new model for requesting assistance from our mentors, SEEs, and SME colleagues. This will enable us to measure their workload and highlight the valuable contributions they make in terms of both collaboration and volume. This will provide greater visibility into the excellent work they are doing.


##Why are we implementing a collaboration model for Mentorship?

As shared in the summary, we want to make sure that the work the Mentors/SMEs/SEEs are putting in is quantifiable and showcases the real impact they have on daily operations.

This will also help them have a better grasp of their workload and correctly assess & prioritize requests.

##What do I need to do?

Every time you will need to engage your mentor/SME/SEE for help on a topic/case/call/analysis of logs please create an **Assistance Request (collaboration)** in DFM.

With the new AR created, ping your mentor/SME/SEE for help.

##What will happen next?

The Mentor/SME/SEE will help you on the task and once everything was handled, he/she will close the collaboration. Please be aware that if the case is closed, the collaboration will be automatically closed as well.

##Do I need a new collaboration every time I ping my mentor even though it is the same case?

Yes! Each new request although might be the same type of request/the same case/same issue needs to have a new Collaboration created.

##When does this start?

Effective Monday the 4th of March, but you can start working in this manner before this date!

##Where can I check who is my aligned TA & mentor?

Please follow the mentorship list that you can find [HERE](https://microsoftapc.sharepoint.com/teams/SCIM-MessagingProtection/Lists/SCIM%20EMEA%20MDO%20%20Mentorship%20Matrix/AllItems.aspx?viewid=691b5b32-cd28-43cf-a8e5-f413b64bd344).

##How do I assign these collaborations to myself as a mentor?

Please do assign the collaborations directly from DfM and **NOT** via the CRM Bot.
