# When To Collab and When To Transfer

## Collab: 3-legged race

- Engineers collaborating on a complex case must coordinate closely to succeed
- If a case only needs one agent's expertise — pass the baton by transferring it
- Customer experience first: Transferring a case to the right person or queue ensures faster, smoother resolution.
- If the case is 98-100% not in your scope transfer it

## Transfer: passing the baton

- Even if you know how to resolve the case, it is best to transfer the case if it is out of scope
- Staffing numbers for the other teams depend on the work that comes to them, so if the case landed in your queue by mistake, reroute it to the correct team without delay
- If the case only needs one scope and collaboration is slowing it down, it is best to transfer it
- Holding onto a misrouted case creates unnecessary wait time and frustration.
