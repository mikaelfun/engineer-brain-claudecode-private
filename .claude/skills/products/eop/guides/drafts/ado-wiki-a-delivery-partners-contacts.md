# Delivery Partner Contact Finder

## Objective

This page provides a **centralized and easy-to-navigate reference** for identifying the right contacts within the Delivery Partner (DP) organization. It covers:

- **Partner Technical Advisors (PTAs)**
- **Service Delivery Managers (SDMs)**
- **Distribution Lists (DLs for PTAs)**

Where applicable, contacts are organized by:
- **Technology** (e.g., Exchange Online, Microsoft Defender for Office, Teams, Intune, etc.)
- **Region** (EMEA, ATZ, APAC)
- **Customer Contract Type** (Premier, ASFP, PSFP, etc.)

## How to Use

1. **Step 1**: Identify the **technology** you need support for.
2. **Step 2**: Filter by **region** (EMEA, ATZ, APAC).
3. **Step 3**: If needed, check the **contract type** (Premier, ASFP, PSFP, etc.).
4. **Step 4**: Use the provided **email DLs** or reach out to the listed PTAs/SDMs directly.
