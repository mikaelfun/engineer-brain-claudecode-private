# Investigating individual App Service slots

## Overview

This quick document explains how to add a slot to AppLens to begin investigation on that specific slot.

## Workflow

Opening individual App Service slots is easy enough, just follow this format: _App Service name(slot name)_.

**Example:** If the App Service is named `myapp` and the slot is named `staging`, enter:
```
myapp(staging)
```
