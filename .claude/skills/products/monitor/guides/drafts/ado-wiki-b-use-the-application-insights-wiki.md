#Overview
___
General instructions on how to use the Application Insights in the most effective way to save time and effort.

#Considerations
___
It was designed to be used in a specific way based around Support Area Paths (SAPs) aka Support Topics.
More training on usage of the wiki and concepts of its use are available in the Internal References section below.
If you have any feedback regarding any page on this wiki, utilize the feedback button at the top right corner to send an email to the administrators of this wiki.

#Step by Step Instructions
___
## Direct Access
1. The wiki was designed to be accessed and consumed based on SAP.
1. Each SAP has two parts the area or branch and specific topic or leaf.
1. Usage is designed to start at the area/branch then move to the specific topic/leaf.
1. Go to the Area/branch page of that SAP shown above
   - [Application Insights Portal Experiences - Overview](https://supportability.visualstudio.com/AzureMonitor/_wiki/wikis/AzureMonitor.wiki/605350/Application-Insights-Portal-Experiences)
1. Going through TSG above will lead to choosing the proper specific topic/leaf, which leads to the specific TSG (e.g., Users and Sessions Investigation Experiences).

## ASC access by Centennial

1. The wiki can be accessed from Centennial Troubleshooters through ASC.
1. Use ASC's Solutions tab (default page), then find the Troubleshooter tile.
1. The Centennial Troubleshooter is a generic wrapper to the wiki itself.
