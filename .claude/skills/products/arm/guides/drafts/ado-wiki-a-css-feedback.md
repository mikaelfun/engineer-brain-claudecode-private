We know that many cases we get could be reduced with changes to the service, improvements to documentation or small tweaks to the service. On the same note, even if the ticket cannot be deflected, there are also areas of improving the support experience when it comes to tooling, TSGs or training.

To provide a way for engineers to share that feedback, we are introducing the following form:

**[[FORMS] PaaS Developer - CSS feedback](https://forms.office.com/r/twpQfXstT8)**

> If there are multiple suggestions about a single ticket, fill in the form as many times as needed.

**Please note this form does not replace the regular escalation process or feedback site for customers**, this is for support engineers, not customers. 

TAs and EEEs will meet to review the feedback provided and prioritize it so that it can be proposed to product group as improvements. 

Please note we cannot speak for product group and provide any warranties that the feedback will be implemented or an ETA when it will happen, but if something comes up for multiple cases, it adds weight to that feedback which makes it more valuable for engineering. That is an ongoing analysis that will happen recurrently.
