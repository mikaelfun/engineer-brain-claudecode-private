## 快速排查路径

1. **iOS devices enrolled via ABM show Azure AD Registered as empty or false in Intune device export. Large number of devices (1400+) affected.**
   → Adopt Just in Time (JIT) deployment for ABM enrollment to ensure proper Azure AD registration. Note: previously affected devices may not be recoverable and may need to be re-enrolled. `[onenote, 🟢 9.5]`

2. **iOS/iPadOS devices display compliance status as '未评估' (Not Evaluated) in Intune admin portal after ABM enrollment.**
   → Use Just in Time (JIT) deployment for new ABM enrollments. Already affected devices likely require re-enrollment. `[onenote, 🟢 9.5]`
