# Intune Backend operations performed by the Intune SAW team

Reference KB: https://internal.evergreen.microsoft.com/en-us/topic/786f051b-aad2-f80e-eb41-e3dcc5a846c5

## Operations performed by the SAW team

- **MDM Authority Reset**
- **Add / Remove flighting tag**
- **Autopilot device deregistration**
- **Destructive tenant move**
- **Add tenant to Intune Canary Program**
- **Enable Coexistence**

> For details on how to request these operations, refer to the KB link above.
