# Intune for Education Guide

## Overview
Cloud-based MDM for educational institutions. Manages classroom devices and apps.

## Portal
https://intuneeducation.portal.azure.com (dedicated portal)

## Licensing
- Microsoft 365 A1 (per-device, one-time)
- Microsoft 365 Education A3/A5
- Standalone Intune for Education subscription
- Office 365 Education plans do NOT include Intune

## Trial
90-day trial available via https://signup.microsoft.com
