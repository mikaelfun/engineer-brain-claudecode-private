# Global DP Business Security Groups

1. **Intune Global PTAs**: This group includes all Intune Partner Technical Advisors and their SDMs.
2. **ConfigManager Global PTAs**: This group comprises all SCCM Partner Technical Advisors and their SDMs.
3. **SCIM Global Management Tools PTAs**: This group holds all Partner Technical Advisors and their SDMs for both Intune and SCCM.
