# Intune Certificate Connector Events and Diagnostic Codes

Reference table of Event IDs (10010-20602) and diagnostic codes (0x00000000-0x0FFFFFFF) for the Intune Certificate Connector. Covers connector service start/stop, certificate renewal, PKCS issue/revoke, upload/download, and CRP verification events.
