# Language Line Interpreter

## Summary

If you have a case with a customer who speaks a different language, you can engage a Language Line interpreter for phone calls.

---

## Option 1: Phone

Dial **US toll free:** (877) 261-6608  
Or **international direct dial:** (831) 648-5860

When prompted for a **Client ID**, enter: **774033**

Select an option:
- Press **1** for Spanish
- Press **2** for All Other Languages
- Press **0** or hold for assistance

The agent will greet you and verify:
- The case number
- Your alias
- The customer's name
- The customer's phone number
- What you're calling about

The agent will then contact the customer and assist with the call.

---

## Option 2: Microsoft Translator

Use: https://translator.microsoft.com
