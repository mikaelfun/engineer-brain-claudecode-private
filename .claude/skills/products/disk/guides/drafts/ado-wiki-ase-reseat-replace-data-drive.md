# Reseating or Replacing a Data Drive on Azure Stack Edge Pro

## Steps

1. If applicable, remove the front bezel covering the drive
2. **Remove the drive**:
   - Press the release button to open the drive carrier release handle
   - While holding the handle, slide the drive carrier out of the drive slot
3. **Wait ~30 seconds**
4. **(Re-)Install the drive**:
   - Insert the drive carrier into the drive slot and slide until it connects with the back plane
   - Close the drive carrier release handle to lock the drive in place
5. If the drive goes online, the green activity LED will flash - drive is ready
