# Internal Guidance: Sharing IcM / Bug / Work Item IDs with Customers

## Scope

Applies to all Delivery Partners (DPs) and all customer-facing communications, including:
- Product limitations
- Identified bugs
- By-design behaviors
- Design Change Requests (DCRs)

## What Is Allowed

- Teams may share the **numerical ID only** (e.g., IcM number, Bug ID, DCR ID)
- Sharing the ID helps:
  - Provide customer confidence that an internal item has been created
  - Indicate that the issue is being tracked internally
  - Support clarity and transparency

> Customers do NOT have access to view or track these items; the numerical ID is shared strictly as a reference.

**CSAM must remain involved** in customer communications where internal IDs are shared.

## What Is NOT Allowed

The following must NOT be shared with customers under any circumstances:
- Internal URLs or links (ADO, IcM, DevOps, etc.)
- Screenshots of internal tools
- Any additional internal metadata or system details

> Even internal links or partial URLs may introduce security risks and must be avoided.

## Documentation Requirement

All related internal references must be documented in the case notes for:
- Case lifecycle traceability
- Audit readiness
- Internal continuity
