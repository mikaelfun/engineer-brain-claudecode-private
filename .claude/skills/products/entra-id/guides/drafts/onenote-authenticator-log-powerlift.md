# Microsoft Authenticator App Log Collection via Powerlift

## Prerequisites
Apply for regional Powerlift entitlement:

| Domain | Entitlement |
|---|---|
| Far East | [IntunePowerLiftFarEast](https://coreidentity.microsoft.com/manage/Entitlement/entitlement/intunepowerl-tew1) |

> **Note**: General Powerlift permission has been revoked by PG. Use regional entitlements.

## Steps to Upload Logs (Customer Side)
1. Open Microsoft Authenticator app
2. Open the top-left hamburger menu
3. Tap **Send Feedback**
4. Tap **Report Issue**
5. Fill in any text for the two questions
6. Tap **Send**
7. An **incident ID (EASYID)** will be displayed - ask customer to screenshot this

## Steps to Collect Logs (Engineer Side)
1. Go to https://aka.ms/powerlift
2. Sign in with your account (must have Powerlift SG access)
3. Click **Incident** in the top menu
4. Enter the EASYID provided by customer
5. Download the diagnostic files
