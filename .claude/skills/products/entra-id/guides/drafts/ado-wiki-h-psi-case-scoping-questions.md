# MS Authenticator PSI Case Scoping Questions

## What

- Is the issue related to registration or authentication using PSI?
- What is the user trying to do when the error occurs?
- Can the user demonstrate the experience? (Video recordings are highly recommended for collaboration.)
- Has PSI ever worked for this user or device?
- Has the user successfully registered PSI in the Microsoft Authenticator app?
- What device type is being used (iOS, Android)?
- Does the device support biometric authentication (e.g., fingerprint, facial recognition)?
- Is PSI enabled for all users or scoped to specific groups?
- Have there been any recent changes to policies, app versions, or configurations?

## When

- When did the issue begin? Was it after a recent update or policy change?
- Is the issue intermittent or reproducible on demand?

## Where

- Does the issue occur when the device is online, offline, or in low-connectivity environments?
- Is the user connected to corpnet, internet, or both?

## Extent

- How many users and devices are impacted?
- Are the affected users part of a specific group, location, or device type?

## General Understanding

- What authentication methods are currently in use within your organization?
- What challenges are you facing with your current password management system?
- What feedback have users provided about the authentication experience?

## Technical Requirements

- Device Compatibility: What types of devices are your users primarily using (e.g., iOS, Android, Windows)?
- Biometric Capabilities: Do your users' devices support biometric authentication (e.g., fingerprint, facial recognition)?
- Infrastructure: What existing infrastructure do you have in place for identity and access management?

## Security Considerations

- Security Concerns: What are your primary security concerns related to authentication?
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA): Are you currently using MFA? If so, what methods are being used (e.g., SMS, email, app-based)?
- Compliance Requirements: Are there any specific compliance requirements your organization needs to meet regarding authentication?
