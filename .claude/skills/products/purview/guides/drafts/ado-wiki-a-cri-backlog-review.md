# CRI Backlog Review Process

Description of the CRI Review backlog process followed by the Supportability Team. Composed of internal tasks routinely followed together with scheduled meetings with CSS and PG stakeholders.

## Internal Supportability Processes

### Every Monday, the Supportability team:
- Performs analysis of SRs +60 days whose status is "Pending the Product Team"
- Results: [Purview Backlog Review.xlsx](https://microsoft.sharepoint.com/:x:/t/DataGovernanceSupportability/ETX8iSX_FLtKtAdcnoTAa2cBQ5r3VAnF-gxV7wLCuZeXkw)
- Updates dashboard: [backlog-pbi - Power BI](https://msit.powerbi.com/groups/me/reports/30fe2e37-9e5d-43dd-9b73-a72b67e45095/e6ce4e3857f85f272888)
- Assesses 'at risk' CRIs via [Project Elevate Dashboard](https://msit.powerbi.com/groups/dbca3772-bb78-4927-9649-bdb9e81413b8/reports/95ef9d89-09a5-448e-8c2e-bbb31671d52f/a1c1fdaa7d50d4b21da4)

### Every day, the Supportability team:
- Tracks aged (60 day+) and S500 CRIs and VIPs +15 days, provides daily updates until conclusion
- Tracks CSS escalation Excel sheet, CSAT Impacting escalation and idleness +7 days via [ICM-PG-Escalation-Tracker.xlsx](https://microsoftapc.sharepoint.com/:x:/t/AzureDataCatalogGen2Engineering-CSSCollaboration/ESqZkwu6SqpAko7Kbn_W2poBTYFmCZF8r-pBWDXU1pU5BA)
- Track CRIs that are open but whose SR is already closed

## Weekly Engagements

- Weekly internal meeting to discuss CRI backlog health
- Weekly meeting with CSS leads to discuss CRI backlog health
- Daily meeting with DG PG to address CRI status
- Weekly meeting with DataSources/DataScan to address CRI status
