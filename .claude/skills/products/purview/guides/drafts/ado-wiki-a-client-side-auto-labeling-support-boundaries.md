Use this Support Area Path (SAP) for support tickets with issues, questions, collaboration, or transfers related to Client issues in Sensitivity labels. It involves the application of these labels within client applications such as Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, PowerPoint), Outlook, and other desktop or mobile apps. When sensitivity labels are applied on the client side, they help classify and protect data based on its sensitivity. For instance, a document labeled as "Confidential" in Word will have restrictions on who can view or edit it, and may also have encryption applied to prevent unauthorized access.              | <a href="https://dev.azure.com/ASIM-Security/Compliance/_wiki/wikis/Information%20Protection/8987/Troubleshooting-Scenarios-Client-Side-Auto-Labeling" target="_blank">Client Side Auto labeling (Opens in new window or tab)</a>                 |
