#WHAT IS Citizen Alliance Support?
The cases with CAS tag belong to a special program called **Citizen Alliance Support** 
It is called **Project Five** as well.

Such kind of case must be serviced by citizens of Australia, New Zealand, Canada, United States, or the United Kingdom

##Reference
[Project Five](https://microsoft.sharepoint.com/sites/knowledgecenter/SitePages/Project-Five.aspx?ls=Ans_Bing)

##Qualified Support Engineers
To be a qualified support engineer for these cases, you need to follow the Training and Clearance section here: https://supportability.visualstudio.com/ProjectBabylon/_wiki/wikis/Microsoft%20Purview/912757/USNAT-Fairfax-Government-Access-Requirements

##Workaround
      
Currently, the Purview Governance team has CAS-qualified engineers only from the AMER shift.

If you are not a citizen of one of the Five Eyes countries and cannot find a proper engineer to handle such a case during your shift, please follow these suggestions:
*   **First, contact the IM of this case.**
Clarify the situation to the IM, explaining that no qualified engineer is available to take the CAS case for the Purview Data Governance team. Request the IM's help in contacting the customer and ask for their preference:

   1.  Opt-out, and the IM could grant temporary access to the current shift engineer. Then engineer with temp access could start work on CAS case.
   2.  Work with the AMER shift and keep CAS support

*   If you have any questions about the action plan, contact the available TA/SME.
*   For the ASIA shift, if no response from IM, or if it is a misrouted case that needs to be transferred directly, you can contact Zhimeng.Zhang@microsoft.com (UTC+8) to help with the SLA. Most of the CAS cases received so far are from AU.
*   Please leave message to @Gustavo Garibay Covarrubias (gustavogar@microsoft.com) for attention. The AMER shift will assist with the following work with the customer if the customer is okay with support during AMER working hours.
