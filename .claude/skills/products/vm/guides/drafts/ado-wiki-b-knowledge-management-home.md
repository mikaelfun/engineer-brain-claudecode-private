# Overview

This page contains information regarding the IaaS Knowledge Management team, its members, processes developed, and more.

# What is the IaaS Knowledge Management (KM) Initiative?

The purpose of the IaaS Knowledge Management (KM) team is to maintain the standards and integrity of the wiki using the processes they developed with a special focus on removing and keeping Personally Identifiable Information (PII) out of all wiki content. The KM team does **not** own the content itself unless otherwise specified.
