#Purpose:
This document outlines the process for Product Group (PG) members to report all the critical issues related to Microsoft Purview, including Live site issues, outages, bugs, and regressions. Prompt and clear reporting allows the EEEs and supportability team to coordinate effectively and provide necessary support.

#Reporting Procedure:
**1. Primary Communication Channel:**

 All outages, critical cases, or significant incidents related to Microsoft Purview should be reported in the dedicated teams channel: **Critical Flash (Bugs, LiveSite, Regressions etc.)**. This is the designated platform for sharing the updates on outages or other high-priority issues so that the relevant teams, including EEEs, are immediately informed and can coordinate a response.

This is the first location where we will look for information regarding any product issue, bug, livesite, regression etc. ALL such incidents should be reported here by the PG.

**Channel:** [Channel link here.](https://teams.microsoft.com/l/channel/19%3A97a053f980c24046982530e36d6638bc%40thread.tacv2/Critical%20Flash%20(Bugs%2C%20LiveSite%2C%20Regressions%20etc.)?groupId=1943848d-4f61-482c-889e-c64a82245fb2)

**2. Email Notification:**

You can also email the Purview EEEs at azurepurvieweees@microsoft.com. This direct notification ensures EEEs are aware of any kind of outages or critical issues. Also this should be used whenever some specific communication or awareness is important to handle the outage.

# Channel and Contact Details:

**Email:** azurepurvieweees@microsoft.com

By following these procedures, we can ensure timely coordination, improve support response times, and enhance Communication to both internal and customers.
