Customer may follow the below steps to raise tickets and contact Purview support

**Step-1:**

Go to Portal, and find "Support + Troubleshooting".
![image.png](/.attachments/image-477bd071-679e-4393-9130-162736ddbb71.png)

**Step-2:**

Enter your issue and click "Go".
![image.png](/.attachments/image-86b190ef-b733-46f6-a6f4-c8c68bb8ba21.png)

**Step-3**:

The self-help service will offer potential solutions for your issue. If these solutions are helpful, please confirm. Should you need further assistance, kindly submit a support request, and a support agent will be in touch with you shortly.

![image.png](/.attachments/image-c183539f-b986-404b-b3ef-d1acfb7623c1.png)


**Step-4**:

In the issue description, kindly ask customers to include the log ID, such as the scan run ID or report ID, to assist with troubleshooting.

![image.png](/.attachments/image-7ca7d234-766b-4137-b7cd-28bc70e80d52.png)
