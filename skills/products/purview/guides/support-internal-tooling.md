## Quick Troubleshooting Path

1. Verify customer is using the account listed as contact for the case. If another person needs access, add them as an extra contact on the case directly... `[source: ado-wiki]`
2. Only original RCRI creator has access via ACL. Known bug: work items 35507191 and 35507130. `[source: ado-wiki]`
3. Log out from ICM portal and log back in to force access refresh. `[source: ado-wiki]`
