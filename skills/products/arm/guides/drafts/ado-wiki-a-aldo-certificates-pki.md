# PKI for disconnected operations

**PKI for disconnected operations** is essential for securing the endpoints provided by the disconnected operations appliance. Public certificate authorities (CA) or enterprise certificate authorities must issue certificates

You create and manage digital certificates to ensure secure communication and data transfer within your Azure Local environment.
