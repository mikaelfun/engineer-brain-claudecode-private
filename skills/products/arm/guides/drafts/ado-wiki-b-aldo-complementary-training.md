###  Overview

While the following courses are not specifically tailored to **Azure Local Disconnected Operations (ALDO)**, they offer **valuable complementary knowledge** that enhances a support engineers understanding of the broader Azure Local ecosystem. These resources cover key areas such as **Azure Stack HCI**, **Windows Admin Center** and **Hybrid/Container orchestration**, all of which intersect with ALDO scenarios in practice.

By engaging with this material, support engineers can:
- Strengthen their foundational and advanced knowledge of Azure Local technologies.
- Gain insights into deployment, networking, and orchestration strategies relevant to hybrid and disconnected environments.
- Improve their ability to troubleshoot and support ALDO-related issues with a broader contextual understanding.
- Stay aligned with evolving Azure capabilities that may influence or integrate with ALDO use cases.
