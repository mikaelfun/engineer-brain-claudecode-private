**Overview**
------------

Azure Local Agents are foundational components that operate as non-clustered, node-local services within Azure Local environments. These agents enable critical infrastructure operations such as VM lifecycle management, telemetry, orchestration, and diagnostics�especially in disconnected or semi-connected scenarios. Unlike clustered services or Azure-managed extensions, these agents are deployed per node and operate independently, ensuring resilience and continuity.

These services are installed per node and operate independently of clustered roles or Azure-managed extensions. They are essential for the orchestration, health, and lifecycle management of Azure Local infrastructure.
