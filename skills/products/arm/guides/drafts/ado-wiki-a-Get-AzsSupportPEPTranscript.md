# CSSTools Get-AzsSupportPEPTranscript

## Synopsis

Compresses the PowerShell transcripts from ERCS and SRNG VMs and stores them in the Azs.Support working directory.

## Examples

```powershell
Get-AzsSupportPEPTranscript
```
