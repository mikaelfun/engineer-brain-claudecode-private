# Intune SME Channels Case Discussion

## How to join "Intune Case Discussion" Teams Channel

1. Open Microsoft Teams App, Select the **Teams** node on the left column
2. Select **"Join Or Create a Team"** in the left corner
3. Enter the following code: **`3q2kyhy`**
4. You will be added as Member of the Team (No Approval required) and you will be able to Post.

## Case Discussion Post Template

After discussing the case with your Tech lead or tier 2 SE, use the following template when creating a post:

```
Case number: XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX

Platforms impacted: XXXXXX

Brief description of the issue:
XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX

What troubleshooting steps have you provided to the customer and what were the results:
XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX

What assistance are you requesting?
XXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXXX
```

## IMPORTANT
- Please be sure to edit your post to remove PII such as UPN, UserID, Device IDs, screenshots etc.
- Until further notice, please have the template information in a log in your case.
- This post should only contain a description of the issue and the case number.
