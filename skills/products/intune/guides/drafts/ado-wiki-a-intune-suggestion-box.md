# Management Solutions Suggestion Box

Management Solutions Suggestion Box is where you can share your ideas on how to make your day-to-day job better.

- Have an idea for a new support tool? Add it!
- Have ideas on improving an existing support tool? Add it!
- Found gaps in content that need to be filled? Add it!
- Want a deep technical dive training into an advanced scenario? Add it!

## Where to Access

**http://aka.ms/MgmtSolsSuggestionBox**

## How It Works

The Intune Embedded Escalation Engineers (EEEs) review all requests that are submitted, evaluate them, and if possible, implement the requested changes. If the changes are something that the EEEs cannot implement themselves directly, they will work with the appropriate stakeholders on your behalf. Emails are sent to the submitter as items are triaged and progress to closure.

> The more information you can provide in the request, and potential impact (if known), will help with prioritization of submissions.
