Since all Command Center tools include prefaces, search for business application: "MC – Quick Assist" when raising tickets. Report the incident by submitting a ticket to Command Center or through the email csscc@microsoft.com.

## Teams for Support

**Current Teams version is not approved for DP engineers** to use to support our customers. See Business Rule: Remote assistance.

**For Delivery Partners**, this project is currently pending a decision from an executive level v-team that's responsible to determine which identities the Delivery Partners will use for:
- Accessing case management tools
- Interacting with customers

**Exception for CE&S AVD users**

At this time, any Support Engineer who has migrated to the CE&S AVD environment is authorized to use Teams for two scenarios:
- **Internal** collaboration, swarming and internal training.
- Join a **customer-initiated Teams meeting**, via the browser-based Teams for audio calls and remote screen viewing when the customer refuses/cannot use Quick Assist.

**NO other use of Teams is authorized**, including but not limited to creating a Teams meeting with a customer, joining a Microsoft-initiated Teams meeting with a customer, taking control of a customer's environment via Teams, or using Microsoft Teams Application for customer support delivery.

The expectations are to continue managing cases using existing approved remote assistance and collaboration tools, and work cases using existing approved processes and tools, as per Remote Assistance policy. The exception applies for web-based Teams in AVD, when customers cannot use Quick Assist.
