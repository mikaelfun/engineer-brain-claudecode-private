# Access to Engineering Wiki (Engineering Hub)

The following steps will grant IntuneCSS (FTE, CSG/DP, Managers) access to the Intune Engineering Wiki, **also referred to as the Engineering Hub**.

> **Note:** Non-CSS engineers/managers will not be approved, such as Cloud Solution Architect (CSA), MCS, CSAM, IM, ACE, Sales, Field, and general Account Team members.

> **IMPORTANT: ALL INFORMATION in any Engineering Wiki is strictly Microsoft-Internal and should never be copied/pasted to any customer-facing communication.**

## Access Request

| Field | Value |
|---|---|
| RBAC Role | Reader |
| Entra ID | Intune Wiki for Support-ReadOnly-9c22 |
| Display Name | Intune Wiki for Support |
| GUID/Alias | 3ce9474b-200e-4b21-825f-126a853eb879 |
| Access Link | https://coreidentity.microsoft.com/manage/Entitlement/entitlement/intunewikifo-oaj0 |

For the **Justification** field, state: ***"Member of CSS requesting ReadOnly access"***

After approval, test with sample link:
https://eng.ms/docs/microsoft-security/management/intune/microsoft-intune/intune/services/autopilot/scenarios/apv2/adminconfigurationflows/devicepreparationprofile

> You will need to minimally logout/log back-in to refresh your security token after you are approved.
