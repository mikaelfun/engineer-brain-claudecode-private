# PTA Contacts

Below you can find a summary from the points of contact from different teams for any necessary discussion regarding case details.

To find contact information for any technology across different teams, please explore the following internal resources:

* **CSS Status** – Use this internal site to search for contact details across different technology teams (TA / PTAS / TM / SDMs)
  * [CSS Status](https://cssstatus.linqto.me)

* **SCIM Compliance & MDO DP** – A central and helpful place to quickly identify the right PTA for different technology areas
  * [S&C SharePoint site](https://microsoft.sharepoint.com/sites/DP-MDOSecurityCompliance/SitePages/PTA-contacts.aspx)
  * [Different Teams contacts list (Excel)](https://microsoft.sharepoint.com/:x:/r/sites/DP-MDOSecurityCompliance/_layouts/15/Doc.aspx?sourcedoc=%7B1BFE013B-292E-4164-81E1-607AE385F93E%7D&file=PTAs%20Contacts.xlsx&action=default&mobileredirect=true)

* **Windows - All PODs** - A useful Excel file listing all the different Windows POD teams (DS; NET; D&D; UEX; SHA; PERF) across all regions (ATZ, EMEA, APAC)
  * [Windows PTAs contacts list](https://microsoftapc.sharepoint.com/:x:/t/WindowsSupportContact/EWyz4PNP2dpPsyc2DZmJKZwB6bY6KYQTBugqh131usnhuQ)
