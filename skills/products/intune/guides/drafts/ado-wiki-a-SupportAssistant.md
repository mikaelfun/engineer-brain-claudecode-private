# About Support Assistant

Currently in Pilot, Support Assistant (Copilot for Microsoft Support Portals) is an AI tool Intune administrators can use to ask support questions without opening a support case

Currently, 5% of all tenants are piloting this feature, 18 of those tenants are Intune customers

This feature is not owned by the Intune team, this page is simply to inform of the feature in the event customers ask questions about it or provide informal feedback during support cases

## How to use Support Assistant

You can find Support Assistant by navigating to "Help and Support" from the "Troubleshooting and Support" blade in the Intune portal
From there, select either Tenant Attach for questions related to Tenant Attach, Windows 365 for questions regarding Cloud PC or Intune for all other Intune related queries

Regardless of your choice, the Support Assistant UX will be the same. The sources Support Assistant uses to answer queries will be different, appropriate to the option you chose.

You can ask plain-language questions in the text field at the bottom of the Support Assistant window and Support Assistant will use knowledge resources and data from the current tenant to provide responses

For example: if you tell Support Assistant you're having trouble enrolling Windows devices, Support Assistant will check your tenant to ensure an MDM authority has been set. If not set, Support Assistant will recommend setting that first before offering further suggestions.

If, after receiving an answer to your question, you'd like to open a support case, Click on the headphones icon at the top of the Window or the link at the bottom of the Window that says "Contact Support"
You'll be given a choice on which contact method you'd like when submitting your support case. Currently, Phone and Email are available. Live chat will be an option in a future release to be determined.
