# **Red Flags**
- Bug or ICM
- By design or product limitation
- Customer behavior or attitude
- Customer not satisfied with action plan/Solution/Work around
- Delay: Collaboration
- Delay: Long running case
- Delay: SLA
- Duplicate case
- Multiple transfers
- Out of scope
- Resolved by Customer
- Scope creep
- Unresolved issue
- Unresponsive customer

# What needs to be done?

Engineers should reach out to their respective TLs who should connect with customers and complete the case recovery and create a better experience before the support case is archived by the Engineer. This would help in creating better customer experience and hence should also improve the survey results of the Engineer.
