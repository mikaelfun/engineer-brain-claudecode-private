# W365 Triage Meeting Process

Engineers/TA/SEE should use the triage form to raise cases in triage meetings.

## Process

1. Submit cases via form (1 day prior to triage): https://aka.ms/w365triageform
2. SaaF team prioritizes submitted cases
3. Cases outside the form addressed based on remaining time

## Triage Form Response List

https://aka.ms/w365triagelist
