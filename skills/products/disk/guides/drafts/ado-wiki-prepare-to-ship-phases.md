## Prepare to Ship Phases — Stuck Troubleshooting Guide

Below are the percentages in which each phase of the prepare to ship process completes:

### Device lock — 0% to 1%

If prepare to ship is stuck at this stage for more than 30 minutes try the following:
- Cancel prepare to ship and try it again
- Cancel prepare to ship, check if the device is showing as locked, if yes, unlock it and try prepare to ship again
- Cancel prepare to ship, check if the device is showing as locked, if yes, keep it locked and try prepare to ship again
- Reboot the device and try prepare to ship again

If none of those solutions solve the problem collect support package and contact management and platform team.

### Checksum calculation — 2% to 80%

If prepare to ship is stuck at this phase, collect the support package and contact Datapath team.

If it is not progressing the Checksum option can be disabled to allow the prepare to ship to continue.

### Rules validation — 81% to 85%

If prepare to ship is stuck at this phase, collect the support package and contact Datapath team.

### BOM publishing — 86% to 90%

Collect support package and contact management and platform team.

### Packaging for BOM — 91% to 95%

Collect support package and contact management and platform team.

### Producing Shipping Label — 95% to 100%

If prepare to ship is stuck at this stage for more than 30 minutes try the following:
- Cancel prepare to ship and try it again
- Cancel prepare to ship, check if the device is showing as locked, if yes, unlock it and try prepare to ship again
- Cancel prepare to ship, check if the device is showing as locked, if yes, keep it locked and try prepare to ship again
- Reboot the device and try prepare to ship again

If none of those solutions solve the problem collect support package and contact management and platform team.
