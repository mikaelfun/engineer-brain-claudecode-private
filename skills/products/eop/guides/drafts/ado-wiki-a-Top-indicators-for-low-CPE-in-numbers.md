# Top Indicators for Low CPE \u2013 In Numbers

Based on customer surveys, the top drivers for **negative CPE results** are:

| Driver | Category | Negative Survey Count | % of surveys referencing this driver |
|--------|----------|-----------------------|--------------------------------------|
| **Resolution** | Resolution time, resolved, unresolved, premature close | 2,834 | 8% |
| **Behavior** | Communication, accountable, resourceful, empathy, efficient | 895 | 3% |
| **Case Progression** | Creation, initial response, SLA, support hours, transfers, case updates, escalations | 746 | 32% |
| **Knowledge** | Scoping, knowledge | 471 | 7% |

**Total surveys analyzed:** ~37,000 (FY22 Q4 to FY24 Q2, Windows Commercial business)

## Key Insight

**Resolution** is the **#1 driver** for negative CPE results, followed by:
1. Behavior
2. Case Progression
3. Knowledge

This clearly shows that getting to a **resolution** (or lack thereof) is what customers care about most. Prolonged cases, unresolved issues, and premature closures are the biggest drivers of dissatisfaction.
