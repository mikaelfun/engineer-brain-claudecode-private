#Case Documentation - Best Practices

_Session was hosted by Alisdair Douglas for EMEA MDO Team_

[[_TOC_]]


##Session Summary

- Keep scope clear and give clear understanding of issue
- Describe business impact in real terms
- Pin both notes at top
- Make sure notes will make it easy for anyone to see current status and progress
- Clear resolution AND pick MDO root cause field
- Link Bug to case
- Change the internal status accordingly (IcMs are automatically added, VSO should be added manually)
- Advice: use internal title to your advantage. Here is an example of an internal title that can help you prioritize your cases: 240227 **WOC**  [ Microsoft GBS ] Internal test case

You can use different set of abbreviations, Andrei shared his best practice in this area:

- WFC - waiting for cx
- WPG - waiting for PG
- TBS - Troubleshooting
- FUP - follow up
