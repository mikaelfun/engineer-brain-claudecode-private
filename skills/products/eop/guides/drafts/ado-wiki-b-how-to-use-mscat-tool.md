# How to use the MSCAT tool

[[_TOC_]]

#Training
To have a better understanding of the tool please watch the training [MS CAPACITY Solution](https://microsofteur.sharepoint.com/teams/MSCapacityTool/SitePages/MS-CAPACITY-TOLL.aspx)

#Obtain access to MSCAT

You can find all the details on this link: [MSCAT request access](https://microsofteur.sharepoint.com/teams/MSCapacityTool/SitePages/FAQ&Support.aspx#how-to-get-access-in-mscat)

How to request profile creation in MSCaT:

- **As a new joiner**: contact your TA or Manager — they will handle the profile creation and assign you to the appropriate team.
- **As an FTE**: Your profile will most likely exist but in cases of long absence the profile will be disabled and deleted after the data retention period has expired.
- **At FTE conversion**: Your data will be kept, and your profile updated with the new userid/email/displayname. You/your manager/your TA need to open a support request for the update to occur.

_MSCaT is conducting access review periodically and will remove access which is not consistently reflected in both your Role and Organization. TA & Managers also have the responsibility to keep this information up to date._


#Book vacation on MSCAT

Access the tool at [MSCAT](https://aka.ms/mscat).

Check the availability of the team by accessing the Team View tab and validate if the capacity is at a white/yellow level. If it is red you must reach to the team to check if there is anyone that could swap, or discuss with TA/Manager.

If the availability is within the expected level, book the vacation by going to the **Request tab**. Choose the date and the type/sub type **"vacation"** and press the **Submit Request**.

This will add the request to the "Show Requests" list, having the state "Pending", "Approved", "Deleted", "Denied", or "Cancelled".

Note: requests in the state "Pending" can be cancelled by the engineer.


#Book training day on MSCAT

Access the tool at [MSCAT](https://aka.ms/mscat).

Check the availability of the team by accessing the Team View tab. If within expected level, book the training days by going to the **Request tab**. Choose the date and the type/sub type **"OutofRota"**, on the sub type choose **"Study Day"** and press the **Submit Request**.
