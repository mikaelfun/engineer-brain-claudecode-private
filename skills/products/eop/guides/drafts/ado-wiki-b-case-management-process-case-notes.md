### Purpose

This process provides a structured approach for managing customer support cases. It includes guidelines for formatting internal titles to track follow-ups, documenting case notes with historical data and action plans, and ensuring all necessary steps are completed before closing a case. The goal is to maintain clear communication, track progress, and ensure thorough resolution of customer issues.

### Expectations

- Pin the case note on every case.
- Maintain and update the case note regularly.

### INTERNAL Title Format:

**Date: Next customer contact | Who has Action – Action Needed | Short description of the customer's issue | Date: Last Updated**

**Example:**

- **Follow up: Aug 2 | Pending PG response for IcM 43645454-Need IcM update | 3 Separate False Positives Events | Last Updated: Aug 1**
- **Follow up: Aug 2 | Pending CX-Implement Domain impersonation Exclusions | Unexpected Email Quarantine | Last update: Aug 1**
- **Follow up: Aug 2 | Pending Intune collab-Collab Follow-up 8/3 | Policy issues | Last Update: Aug 1**
