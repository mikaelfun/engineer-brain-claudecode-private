# Details Required for Initiating a Remediation ICM

When opening a remediation ICM, include the following information:

## Required Information

1. **Approval ID** or **Remediation ID**
2. **PSR and HAR file** that reproduce the issue
   - See [HAR & Browser Trace Playbook](https://dev.azure.com/ASIM-Security/Messaging%20Protection/_wiki/wikis/Messaging%20Protection%20Wiki/13973/HAR-Browser-Trace-Playbook)

## Finding the Approval ID

Located at the end of creating a remediation action:
- Action Center page > click Remediation > check right panel

## Finding the Remediation ID

Located on the Action Center page:
- Click Remediation > appears in the right panel
