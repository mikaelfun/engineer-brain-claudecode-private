### 排查步骤
1. **Data Storage**: What data does MDC store? How long retained? Encrypted at rest and in transit?
2. **Data Residency**: Which regions? Can customers choose? Is data replicated across regions?
3. **Privacy**: Who has access? GDPR/CCPA compliance? Third-party data sharing?
