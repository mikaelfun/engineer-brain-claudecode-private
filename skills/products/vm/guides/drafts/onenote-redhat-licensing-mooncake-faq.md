# Redhat Licensing FAQ for Mooncake (Azure China)

> Source: OneNote MCVKB 2.6 | Draft - pending SYNTHESIZE review

## Key Points

- Red Hat images on Azure China are provided by **Yungoal** (third-party partner)
- Red Hat subscriptions are NOT included in VM pricing - must be purchased separately
- Global Red Hat agreements do NOT apply to Azure China - separate subscription required from Yungoal/Red Hat China
- Contact: redhat@yungoal.com

## FAQ

### Q: Does Azure China VM pricing include Red Hat licensing?
A: No. You need to buy Red Hat subscriptions separately from Yungoal.

### Q: Can I use global Red Hat licenses in Azure China?
A: No. Azure China requires separate subscriptions from Red Hat China (Yungoal) due to regulatory compliance.

### Q: How to get Yungoal MPN (Microsoft Partner Number)?
A: Contact Yungoal directly for MPN details.

### Q: Can I bring my own Red Hat subscription (BYOS)?
A: Yes, if you have unused NEW Red Hat subscriptions. Put Yungoal's MPN into your Azure Account. However, support for Linux issues goes to where you purchased the subscription.

### Q: What type of subscription is recommended?
A: Certified Cloud and Service Provider (CCSP) subscriptions. CCSP Yum servers are Azure-based for better performance.

### Q: Is Yungoal's image different from official Red Hat?
A: No technical difference. Yungoal converts official Red Hat images to Azure-compatible format, same as Azure Global (also a CCSP partner).

### Q: Can I upgrade Red Hat versions on Azure China?
A: Yes, Yungoal's subscriptions allow updates/upgrades.

## Support Scope
- Image/OS issues with Yungoal subscription -> Contact Yungoal
- Image/OS issues with external subscription -> Contact original vendor
- Azure platform issues -> Microsoft Support
