# Lifecycle Workflow Insights

Workflows created using Lifecycle Workflows provide administrators the automation of lifecycle tasks for users in the Joiner-Mover-Leaver (JML) model. With Workflow Insights, administrators can get a quick view of workflow execution within the environment.

## Numerical summaries

The workflow summary view provides a quick overview of:
- Successful Workflows count
- Users Processed count
- Successful Tasks count

Over spans of 7, 14, and 30 days.

## Top Workflows by Runs

Filter to see the top workflows by Total Processed, Successful, or Failed Workflows.

## Top Tasks by Runs

Filter to see Total Processed tasks, Successful Tasks, Failed Tasks, Total Users, Successful Users, and Failed Users. Filterable for 7, 14, or 30 days.

## Workflow Runs by Category

View top workflows run by category (Joiner/Mover/Leaver) using percentages for 7, 14, or 30 days. Filterable by Total Processed, Successful, and Failed.

## Teams Channel

Support Engineers and the LCW product group communicate via the **Lifecycle Workflows** Teams channel under **Cloud Identity - Account Management** (AVA aware).
