# General
---
The wiki is designed to be accessed from our Centennial Troubleshooters accessed through ASC.

Use ASC's Solutions tab (default page), using this icon and then off to the left on the resulting pane you find a Troubleshooter tile and clicking it will bring up the troubleshooter link.

# Specifics
---
The wiki is structured in a manner to provide a top down approach on issue using the smallest amount of information necessary to achieve specifically desired goals. As a result there are a number of tiered articles that will reference other articles as needed.

If you have any feedback regarding any page on this wiki, utilize the feedback button at the top right corner to send an email to the administrators of this wiki.
