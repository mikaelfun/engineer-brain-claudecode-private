How to get the ResourceId value of an Azure resource from Azure Support Center

Instructions:
1. Follow the steps in How to navigate to a resource in Azure Support Center
2. With the desired resource selected, click the Properties tab
3. Hover the mouse just to the right of the resource name until a copy icon appears then click to copy the ResourceId to clipboard

Example: /subscriptions/00000000-0000-0000-0000-000000000000/resourceGroups/Testing1/providers/microsoft.insights/metricalerts/Percentage CPU greater than 80
