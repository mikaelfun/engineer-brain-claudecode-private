# Overview
---
- Determining if an application was updated is key item to know when things stop working like the used to
- AppLens offers several detectors to look for application deployments of App Service applications like web apps and functions.

# General Guidance
---
1. Go to AppLens
1. In the search box in the upper left type, *deployment*
1. This will surface known detectors — use these to check recent deployment history and correlate with the time telemetry or behavior changed
