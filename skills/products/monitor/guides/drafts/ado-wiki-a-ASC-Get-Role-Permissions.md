How to get permissions assigned to a role in Azure Support Center

Instructions:
1. Open Azure Support Center from support request
2. Navigate to Resource Explorer
3. Select any resource or the subscription itself
4. With the desired resource selected, click the Access Control tab
5. Under the Roles section will be a list of all the roles in the subscription
6. Type the role name (or partial name) into the Name filter and press Enter to filter the list
7. Locate your desired role in the list and expand to see the permissions applied to that role
